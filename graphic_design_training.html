🎯 Digital Skill Test
Build an App Prototype!

Throughout the course, students will spend a considerable amount of time using the Figma app and its many features. Your assignment is to create a prototype of an app's home screen. You are free to be creative with the app's theme, as long as your home screen meets the following criteria:

Requirements
1

Show your font

The home screen must mention the font's name. The font must stylistically match the illustration.

2

Draw a vector illustration

The page must feature a simple vector illustration drawn in Figma using the Pen tool.

3

Add an interactive home button

The page must include a home button that changes colour when the cursor hovers over it. Keep in mind that this feature must be implemented using Components. You can use this tutorial for guidance:

▶ Components Tutorial →
4

Present it on an iPhone

The prototype must be presented on the iPhone 14/15/16. Be sure to follow the dimensions.

Sharing your result
5

Share your Figma prototype link

Finally, share your result by sending the link to your Figma prototype, as shown in this example:

🔗 View Example Prototype →

Here you can find a tutorial on how to share the Figma link:

▶ How to Share a Figma Link →
⚠️ Important — please read carefully
  • If you violate several key requirements at once, there may be no opportunity to revise the project.
  • The project must NOT be fully or partially copied from the internet — doing so will immediately raise concerns about your honesty and professionalism.
  • 🚫 The use of AI is a direct rejection reason. Completing the final project on your own not only proves you have the skills to teach — it shows you value your own learning.

Complete the skill test form and paste your project link as the answer to the final question.
